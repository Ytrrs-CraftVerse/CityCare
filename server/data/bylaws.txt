# CityCare Municipal Bylaws & Resolution Matrix

## Section 1: Roads & Potholes
1.1 The city is strictly responsible for fixing potholes and structural road damage on PUBLIC streets and highways.
1.2 The city is NOT responsible for potholes, sinkholes, or damage inside private driveways, private parking lots, or gated communities. These fall under private property ownership.
1.3 Priority 5 (Emergency) dispatch is required if a pothole is deeper than 10 inches on a major arterial road.

## Section 2: Flora & Tree Maintenance
2.1 The city will prune or remove fallen trees ONLY if they fall on public roads, public parks, or municipal power lines.
2.2 If a tree falls inside a citizen's private yard, it is the sole responsibility of the homeowner to clear it, even if it causes minor damage to their private structure.
2.3 Any tree roots cracking a city sidewalk are the responsibility of the city. 

## Section 3: Water & Plumbing
3.1 The city waterworks department handles all pipe bursts, water main breaks, and sewage overflows that occur on the main street or public utility lines.
3.2 The city is NOT responsible for plumbing issues, pipe leaks, or sewage backups that occur within the boundary of a private residence (past the water meter into the house). Homeowners must call a private plumber.

## Section 4: Garbage & Waste
4.1 The city handles illegal dumping on public property and overflow of municipal bins.
4.2 Large furniture or construction debris dumped on private property must be handled by the property owner at their own expense.
